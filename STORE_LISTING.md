## Description (full)
Big Board Chess brings free, ad-free online chess to the living room. Pull up a controller and play
real people around the world, follow live games, sharpen your tactics, and study your openings — all
on the big screen.

• Play online — rated and casual games at any time control, or challenge a friend
• Play the computer — pick your level when you just want a quiet game
• Watch live — follow top games and broadcasts as they happen
• Puzzles — train tactics with a never-ending stream of positions
• Analysis board — engine evaluation, the opening explorer, and endgame tablebases
• Studies — open and step through any public study, chapter by chapter
• Board editor & coordinate trainer — set up positions and learn the squares
• Make it yours — board themes and piece sets

Sign in with your existing lichess account in seconds (scan a code with your phone — no typing),
or play the computer without an account.

Powered by lichess.org. **Unofficial — not affiliated with or endorsed by lichess.org.**
