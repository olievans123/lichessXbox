# Privacy Policy — Big Board Chess

_Last updated: 13 June 2026_

Big Board Chess ("the app") is an unofficial chess client for playing on
[lichess.org](https://lichess.org). This policy explains what the app does and
does not do with your information. **The app's developer does not operate any
servers, does not run analytics or advertising, and does not collect, sell, or
share your personal data.**

## What the app stores
- **Your lichess sign-in token.** If you sign in, the app receives an OAuth
  access token from lichess and stores it **only on your device**, in the
  Windows Credential Vault. It is never sent to the developer. You can remove it
  at any time by signing out in the app.
- **Your settings** (board theme, piece set, sound preference) are saved locally
  on your device.

That's all that's retained. The app keeps no account on any developer-run
server because there is none.

## Who the app talks to
To work, the app connects directly to these third-party services. Your use of
them is governed by their own privacy policies:

- **lichess.org** — all gameplay, puzzles, studies, tournaments, live games,
  and account data come from the official lichess API. See the
  [lichess privacy policy](https://lichess.org/privacy).
- **api.qrserver.com** — when you sign in, the app sends the lichess
  authorization URL to this service to render the on-screen QR code you scan
  with your phone. The URL is not secret and contains no password.
- **jsdelivr CDN (cdn.jsdelivr.net)** — used to fetch optional chess-piece
  artwork and the analysis engine when they aren't bundled in the app.

During sign-in, the app also runs a small temporary web server on your local
network so the phone you authorize with can return the sign-in code to your
console. This stays on your home network and is not exposed to the internet.

## What the app does not do
- No advertising, no ad networks, no trackers.
- No analytics or telemetry.
- No collection of your name, email, location, contacts, or payment information.
- No sharing or selling of any data.

## Children
The app is a general-audience chess client and is not directed at children.
